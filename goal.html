Your data never touches the internet

Most “smart homes” send everything to a cloud server – which means someone else’s computer knows when you sleep, when you’re away, even what you say. We reject that. SASH runs on a local server inside your home. No external connection, no subscription fees, no surveillance. Even our updates are opt‑in and peer‑to‑peer. Security is our #1 priority – not convenience at the cost of privacy.
